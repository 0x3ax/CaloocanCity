Taoist Temple (5th avenue)



The Thai To Taoist Temple

Located near the 5th Ave LRT station, youâ€™ll find a Taoist temple over 100 years old. This is no ordinary Taoist temple, however. The architects of this temple went all out to create a 3-story masterpiece.

You can certainly see how this temple has aged with paint that has begun to . off and the cement walls could use a good power wash. This slight dilapidation, however, contributes to the intrigue of such an ancient building.

This temple has been a place of worship for the Chinese and the Chinese-Filipino residents of Caloocan for almost a century.
